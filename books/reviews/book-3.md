# AI Superpowers: China, Silicon Valley, and the New World Order

Kai-Fu Lee, a renowned AI expert with experience in both Chinese and American tech ecosystems, offers an unparalleled perspective on the global AI competition. The book explores how China's unique approach to AI development differs from Silicon Valley's innovation model and what this means for the future global order.

Lee's insights into China's AI ambitions and capabilities are particularly valuable, challenging many Western assumptions about the AI race. The book serves as both a wake-up call and a roadmap for understanding how artificial intelligence is reshaping global power dynamics and economic structures.
