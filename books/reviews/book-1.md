# AI 2027

A thought-provoking exploration of the near-future AI landscape, co-authored by Daniel Kokotajlo and Scott Alexander. This book examines the emergence of autonomous AI agents and their profound impact on society, technology, and global power dynamics.

The authors present a compelling narrative about the accelerating AI arms race and the critical decisions humanity faces as artificial intelligence systems gain unprecedented capabilities. Their analysis combines technical insight with geopolitical awareness, making it essential reading for anyone interested in the future of AI development.
